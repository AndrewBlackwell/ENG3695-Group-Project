Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions to get your VORTEX-ONE drone ready for its first flight.

Unboxing & Preparation

1

Check Package Contents

Verify that all components are included: drone body, controller, propellers, batteries, charging hub, cables, and documentation.

2

Remove Protective Elements

Remove the gimbal cover, battery insulation tabs, and all protective films. Keep the gimbal cover for storage.

3

Charge Everything

Fully charge the drone battery and controller before first use. A complete charge takes approximately 90 minutes.

Software Setup

4

Download VORTEX Fly App

Install the VORTEX Fly app on your mobile device from the App Store or Google Play Store. Create or log in to your VORTEX account.

5

Connect Controller & Phone

Attach your mobile device to the controller using the provided cable. Different cables are included for various phone models.

6

Activate & Update

Follow the in-app instructions to activate your VORTEX-ONE and install the latest firmware updates. This ensures optimal performance and features.

Pre-Flight Tips

Before Your First Flight

  • Choose an open area free from obstacles and away from crowds for your first flights
  • Check local regulations and airspace restrictions before flying
  • Always perform a compass calibration before your first flight in a new location
  • Set your Return-to-Home altitude to safely clear any obstacles in the area
  • Start with Beginner Mode enabled to limit speed and distance while learning
  • Monitor battery levels carefully - return when they reach 30% for safe landing
  • Keep your drone within visual line of sight at all times
